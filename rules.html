The Guessing Game

The Rules

1. When the game begins, I will pick a random number between 1-100.

2. You will have 5 opportunities to find this number.



3. Every time you guess, I will tell you whether you are super cold, cold, hot, super hot.

Super cold > 40
Cold > 30
Hot < 20
Super Hot < 10

4. Don't worry if you lose, because you can just try.



So can you guess my number?
