The Guessing Game

The Rules

1. When the game begins, I will pick a random number between 1-100.

2. You will have 5 opportunities to find this number.

3. Every time you guess, I will tell whether you are super cold, cold, hot, super hot.

4. Super cold > 40, Cold > 30, hot < 20, super hot < 10

5. Don't worry if you lose, because you can just try.

6. So can you guess my number?
